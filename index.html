Giới Thiệu

The NVIDIA RTX 50 series, powered by the cutting-edge **Blackwell architecture**, represents a significant leap forward in graphics processing. Unveiled at CES 2025, these GPUs are designed to redefine performance for gamers, creators, and AI professionals. A core innovation is the **Blackwell architecture** itself, featuring more efficient transistors, increased core counts, and a substantial boost in memory bandwidth thanks to the adoption of **GDDR7 memory**. This allows the RTX 50 series to handle the most demanding tasks with unprecedented ease. For instance, the flagship RTX 5090 is reported to deliver up to 50% higher frame rates at 4K resolution compared to its predecessor. Beyond raw performance, the RTX 50 series places a heavy emphasis on **AI acceleration**. It introduces **DLSS 4.0**, with advanced features like Multi Frame Generation and transformer-based models, significantly improving framerates and image quality through AI-driven upscaling. New AI hardware is integrated to boost performance across various workloads, and an AI management processor (AMP) helps optimize task allocation. The lineup spans from the enthusiast-grade **RTX 5090** and **RTX 5080** (launched in January 2025) to the **RTX 5070 Ti**, **RTX 5070**, **RTX 5060 Ti**, and **RTX 5060** (rolling out through February to June 2025). Mobile variants are also available, bringing Blackwell's power to laptops. The RTX 50 series is poised to set new standards in gaming realism, content creation, and AI-driven applications.
